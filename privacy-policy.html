Privacy Policy for Amber Design Studio

Amber Design Studio

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Amber Design Studio ("we," "us," or "our") handles user data across all applications and products we develop, including our watch faces (e.g., "Legacy Classic Watch Face", "The Stellar Horizon Watch Face"), our utility applications, and our games (e.g., "Smash Blocks").

At Amber Design Studio, we deeply value our users' privacy. While we do not directly collect, store, or harvest your personal information on our own servers, some of our applications utilize trusted third-party SDKs, advertising networks, and payment processors to provide core functionalities, ads, and in-app purchases.

2. Data Handling and Processing

Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

We do not directly collect or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other information that could personally identify you.

Location and Sensor Data

Some of our applications and watch faces access your device's approximate or precise location and sensor data (such as compass/gyroscope) to provide real-time weather updates, air quality indexes, or orientation metrics. This data is processed ephemerally (locally on your device) or transmitted directly to trusted third-party data providers/SDKs to fetch current information. We do not track, store, or share your location history on our own servers.

Local Storage

Any settings, preferences, or offline game progress (e.g., high scores in "Smash Blocks") are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

3. Third-Party Services, Advertising, and In-App Purchases

To support our development and provide robust app features, our applications may integrate third-party software development kits (SDKs), APIs, and services. These third parties may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

A. Third-Party Data SDKs and APIs

Our utility and weather applications integrate external APIs/SDKs to retrieve live weather, air quality, and celestial data. Data sent to these providers is strictly limited to what is necessary to fulfill the data request (such as localized coordinates) and is handled ephemerally.

B. Advertising Networks

Some of our free applications and games (such as "Smash Blocks") display third-party advertisements (e.g., Google AdMob). These advertising partners may collect and use device identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID), IP addresses, and usage data to serve relevant ads, limit ad repetition, and measure ad performance. You can manage your ad personalization choices within your Android device settings.

C. In-App Purchases and Billing

Our games may offer digital content or features via in-app purchases. All financial transactions are processed securely by the Google Play Store Billing system. Amber Design Studio does not collect, access, or store your credit card numbers, billing addresses, or financial data. Google provides us only with anonymous transaction confirmations and purchase tokens to unlock your digital assets.

Note on Third-Party Policies: We encourage you to review the privacy policies of our primary service providers:

  • Google Play Services & AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Application Permissions

Our applications only request permissions that are strictly necessary for their core functionalities (such as internet access for weather retrieval or location access for local climate data). If an application requires a specific permission, it will request it transparently at runtime. You can revoke these permissions at any time via your device settings, though doing so may limit certain features.
